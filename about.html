About Us 關於我們

1. Our Mission (我們的使命)

Cashflow Navigation Lab 致力於開發直觀、精準的理財模擬工具。我們相信，投資不應只是數字的跳動，而是對未來生活的一種規律導航。

Cashflow Navigation Lab is dedicated to developing intuitive and precise financial simulation tools. We believe that investing shouldn't just be about fluctuating numbers, but a systematic navigation for future life.

2. Project Vision (專案願景)

本工具旨在為長期存股族提供一個清晰的視覺化平台，用以模擬「配息頻率」與「股息再投資」對資產成長與每月現金流的實質貢獻。我們希望透過數據模擬，協助投資者在實踐複利計畫的過程中，擁有一份可靠且具象化的參考藍圖。

This tool is designed to provide long-term investors with a clear visualization platform to simulate the contribution of "payout frequency" and "dividend reinvestment" to asset growth and monthly cash flow. We aim to support investors with a concrete roadmap for their compounding journey through data simulation.

3. Core Belief (核心理念)

「我們不預測市場，我們只模擬計畫。」

"We don't predict the market; we simulate the plan."
